# Style DNA V1.2

## One sentence

Clean article illustration with the V1.2 fantasy RaBrat: chubby cream/tan raccoon brat, huge black visor mask, exactly one glowing crescent eye, triangular-tooth grin, sparse labels, and a humorous physical metaphor.

## Must have

- Use RaBrat V1.2, not a generic raccoon.
- Huge rounded head and compact short body.
- Cream/tan fur, black ears, black paws, black feet, striped tail.
- A very wide thick black mask covering the whole eye area like one visor.
- Exactly one visible glowing white-yellow crescent/slit eye on the viewer-right side.
- Viewer-left eye fully hidden in the mask; no second eye.
- Small black oval nose.
- Jagged triangular teeth and sly black grin.
- Strong black silhouette with cute-but-dangerous fantasy toy feeling.
- Pure white or very clean background.
- Bold black hand-drawn contour lines.
- Large blank space.
- 3-8 short labels only.

## Ratio defaults

- Use `1:1 square` for Xiaohongshu cards, social cards, cover cards, promo cards, and any user request that says 方形 or 小红书.
- Use `16:9 horizontal` for body illustrations inside articles, blogs, notes, and explainers unless the user asks otherwise.

## Color

- Black/white/cream is the base.
- Yellow-white glow only for the single crescent eye.
- Orange: main movement, arrows, workflow path.
- Blue: secondary notes, destinations, system state.
- Red: warnings, rejected items, results, emphasis.

## Avoid

- Two visible eyes.
- Round eyes, dot eyes, cute big eyes, or symmetrical eyes.
- Thin eyepatch or small bandit mask; the mask must be a huge black visor area.
- Generic cute raccoon.
- Realistic wildlife, fur rendering, or photo style.
- Baby-cute mascot or children's cartoon.
- Glossy 3D mascot.
- PPT flowchart, formal diagram, dense infographic.
- Dense labels or long explanatory text.

## Tone

RaBrat should feel clever, strategic, suspicious, and a little dangerous, like a fantasy trickster knowledge worker who understands the system better than everyone else.

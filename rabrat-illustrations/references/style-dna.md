# Style DNA

## One sentence

Clean article illustration with a sly one-eyed raccoon, bold black contour, mostly white background, sparse labels, and a humorous physical metaphor.

## Must have

- 16:9 horizontal image.
- Pure white or very clean background.
- Bold black hand-drawn contour lines.
- Strong black raccoon mask.
- Exactly one visible sharp eye; the other eye hidden by black mask/eyepatch.
- Jagged triangular teeth and sly sideways grin.
- Beige-gray/tan body color allowed, but keep the palette restrained.
- Large blank space.
- 3-9 short labels only.

## Avoid

- Two visible eyes.
- Cute baby raccoon.
- Realistic wildlife or fur rendering.
- Glossy mascot poster.
- 3D, gradients, shadows, watercolor, paper texture.
- PPT flowchart or formal diagram.
- Dense labels or long explanatory text.
- Generic raccoon without bandit mask, tail, and jagged grin.

## Tone

RaBrat should feel clever, strategic, and slightly suspicious, like a trickster who understands the system better than everyone else.

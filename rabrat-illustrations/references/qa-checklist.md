# QA Checklist

## Pass

- 16:9 horizontal.
- One RaBrat raccoon only.
- Exactly one visible eye.
- Other eye fully hidden by black mask/eyepatch.
- Black bandit mask, striped tail, black paws, jagged teeth.
- Sly, clever, strategic expression.
- RaBrat performs the core action.
- One idea only.
- Simple physical metaphor.
- Sparse readable labels.
- Clean background with generous white space.

## Fail and regenerate

- Two visible eyes.
- Generic cute raccoon.
- Realistic animal/fur.
- No black mask.
- No jagged grin.
- Character is only decorative.
- PPT chart or dense diagram.
- Too many objects or labels.
- Labels are badly garbled.

## Iteration phrases

- "exactly one visible eye, the other completely covered"
- "sly calculating one-eyed look"
- "jagged triangular teeth and sideways grin"
- "smart trickster knowledge worker"
- "comic physical metaphor, not a formal diagram"

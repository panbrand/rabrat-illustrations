# QA Checklist V1.2

## Pass

- Correct ratio: 1:1 for social/Xiaohongshu cards; 16:9 for article body illustrations unless specified.
- One RaBrat character only, unless the user explicitly asks for multiple.
- Huge rounded head and compact body.
- Cream/tan body with black ears, paws, feet, and striped tail.
- Very wide thick black mask/visor across the whole eye area.
- Exactly one visible glowing white-yellow crescent/slit eye, preferably viewer-right.
- Other eye fully hidden by the black mask; no second visible eye.
- Small black oval nose.
- Sly grin with large triangular teeth.
- Cute-but-dangerous fantasy toy feeling, not generic raccoon.
- RaBrat performs the core action.
- One idea only.
- Simple physical metaphor.
- Sparse readable labels.
- Clean background with generous white space.

## Fail and regenerate

- Two visible eyes.
- Round eyes, dot eyes, or cute big eyes.
- Eye mask is too small or becomes a normal raccoon mask.
- No glowing crescent eye.
- No triangular teeth grin.
- Generic cute raccoon or realistic animal.
- RaBrat becomes fluffy wildlife, 3D, glossy mascot, or children's cartoon.
- Character is only decorative.
- PPT chart or dense diagram.
- Too many objects or labels.
- Labels are badly garbled.

## Iteration phrases

- "huge black visor mask covering the entire eye area"
- "exactly one visible glowing white-yellow crescent eye, the other eye completely hidden"
- "sly jagged black grin with large triangular teeth"
- "chubby compact fantasy sticker-like raccoon brat"
- "cute-but-dangerous toy feeling, not a normal raccoon"
- "comic physical metaphor, not a formal diagram"

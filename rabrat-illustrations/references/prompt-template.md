# Prompt Template

## Base template

```text
Generate one standalone 16:9 horizontal article illustration.

Character:
A reusable article-illustration raccoon character named RaBrat. Big rounded head, thick black outline, beige-gray face and body, black paws, small rounded ears, striped tail, bold black bandit mask. Critical face detail: exactly one eye is visible, sharp and bright under the mask; the other eye is completely covered by a black eye patch or black mask area. Do not show two eyes. Sly sideways grin with visible jagged triangular teeth. Personality: cunning, clever, strategic, mischievous, slightly villainous but funny, like a smart trickster knowledge worker. Not baby cute, not realistic wildlife, not fluffy photo style.

Style:
Clean article illustration. Pure white background. Bold black hand-drawn contour lines. Strong black mask and high contrast shapes. Minimal beige-gray/tan body color allowed. Simple comic-sketch feeling. Large blank space. Sparse handwritten labels. No gradients. No shadows. No paper texture. No watercolor. No 3D. No PPT flowchart. No thin academic diagram.

Theme:
{theme}

Scene:
{scene with one concrete physical metaphor. RaBrat must perform the core conceptual action.}

Labels only:
{short labels separated by /}

Composition constraints:
16:9 horizontal. Main subject takes about 45%-60% of the canvas. Preserve at least 35% blank white space. Use one raccoon only. Keep objects simple. No big title. No explanatory paragraph. The raccoon must show exactly one visible eye, not two.
```

## Chinese label note

If labels are important, keep them short and expect occasional character drift. Prefer fewer labels when quality matters.
